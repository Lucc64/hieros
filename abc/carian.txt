Cities Andanus, Myndus, Bybassia, Larymna, Chysaoris, Alabanda, Plarasa and Iassus were puzzling to the Greeks, some of whom attempted to give etymologies in words they said were Carian.

The Greek is:[7]

    Σῆμα τόδε: Τυρί
    Καρὸς τὸ Σκύλ[ακος]

The translation is:

  This is the tomb of Tur 
  the Carian, the son of Scylax

  ZUM TODE TYR
  Care de Scyl'akus

The first line is repeated in Carian:

    Śías san Tur

This san[ktuary] Tyr
Śías = This  τόδε = That

The Carian cemetery of Delos probably represents the pirates mentioned in classical texts.
