Dialects of the Egyptian language were spoken longer than any other known language. Roughly from 3500BC to 500AD. After only a few centuries the writing system converged to a form which remained mostly unchanged for more than 3000 years and was still in use near the end of the Roman aera.
Hiero
1. Holy ( ἱερός, hero,… )
2. Old (gero,γέρω,year-old, …)
